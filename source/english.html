about us

penelopa

PENELOPA is a group of professionals whose main goal is to implement client ideas.

We carry the finest contract sewing in Belarus. We located in Brest but work with customers from the European Union and the Russian Federation. The production capacity of our company allows us to produce up to 10,000 units per month.

Our services will be of interest to :

  • large fabricators
  • creative designers creating signature collections
  • We will help design and create business clothes for your employees.
  • Also our company is ready to collaborate with hotels, restaurants, boarding houses and recreation centers.

The technological equipment of the company is continually updated to ensure the smooth production process.

Responsible approach to our work guarantees the quality and safety of products.
